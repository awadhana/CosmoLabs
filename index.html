About CosmoLabs

Founded by engineers. Run like a mission.

CosmoLabs was founded by engineers who spent their careers inside demanding programs — shipping cloud platforms at global scale, integrating flight hardware, and defending reactor safety cases. We started the firm with a simple conviction: mission-critical work deserves consultants who have actually done the work.

We combine startup agility with national-laboratory rigor. Our teams move in weeks, not quarters — yet every deliverable, whether an architecture review, a GNC simulation campaign, or a licensing submittal, is engineered to withstand board-level and regulator-level scrutiny.

Today CosmoLabs serves CTOs, CIOs, and program managers across commercial and federal programs, acting as the trusted technical bridge between ambitious roadmaps and the engineering reality required to deliver them.
