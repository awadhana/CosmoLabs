## Intake wizard (`#start`)

1. **Brief** — required: title (3–200), description (10–5000), work email, **phone (required — unusual first-touch B2B friction)**.
2. **Details** — all optional: ≤5 reference URLs, light/dark, ≤6 brand colors, files (3 × 1.5 MB, 3 MB total, whitelist + magic-byte checks).
3. **Clarify** — AI questions. With `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY` unset (**current prod state**): instant localized fallback questions (audience / assets / timeline & budget), no network, no PoW. With key set: spinner + transparent PoW solve + `POST /api/clarify` (captcha-gated, time-gated, rate-limited 6/10 min/IP).
4. **Review** — read-only summary + **mandatory human check** (Turnstile if configured, else the visible PoW "I'm not a robot" ~30k-hash solve) + consent line → submit.

Wizard friction worth quantifying (once measurement exists):

- Required phone at step 1; Enter never advances a step; no draft persistence (reload loses everything).
- Mandatory visible PoW click at step 4; hard-fails permanently on browsers without WebCrypto; IP-bound solutions break on mobile network handoff; 15-min expiry.
- **Silent false-positive lead destruction:** honeypot autofill or submit <3 s after `startedAt` (client clock vs server clock!) returns fake success `int_0` and stores nothing — the prospect sees a success screen, nobody is notified, and this branch emits **no log** (api/intake.js:205-216, 308-311).
- Rate limits charged before validation (intake 8/10 min/IP) — retries after validation errors burn quota; corporate NAT shares buckets.
- Success screen ends at "wait for our email": no booking link, no status lookup, no way to append to a reference id.

## Post-submission pipeline (what happens to a lead)

`/api/intake` gauntlet in order: POST → JSON → size (413 >4.5 MB) → same-origin → rate limit (429) → shape → **silent bot-drop** → validation (400 `validation`) → human check (400 `turnstile`/`captcha`) → blob storage (files first, manifest last) → best-effort fan-out: owner email + localized (EN/FR/AR) customer confirmation via Gmail SMTP, then Discord embed with signed Approve/Skip links (7-day TTL). Forge lifecycle: `new → approved → building → preview → done` (+`dropped`/`build_failed`); Promote emails the client their live URL (English-only, unlike the confirmation).

Known leak points after conversion:

- Email fan-out is fire-and-forget: bad Gmail creds → owner never notified, customer never confirmed, nothing persisted, no retry.
- Discord webhook down/unset → no Approve link ever delivered; links expire in 7 days with no re-mint endpoint; no cron/poller sweeps stale `new` briefs.
- Skip and `build_failed` send the prospect **nothing** — contradicting the one-business-day promise they were emailed.
- No CRM, no drip/nurture, no booking/scheduling anywhere, no client-facing status lookup, no newsletter or low-commitment capture path (all grep-verified absences).

## Highest-signal gaps (ranked, as of this snapshot)

1. **Zero measurement** — nothing else can be quantified until this exists.
2. **#contact form is a mailto dead-end** — corporate machines without a mail handler lose the lead after they filled three fields; "Opening your email app" shows even when nothing opens.
3. **Silent bot-drop false positives** destroy real leads with no log and a fake success screen.
4. **No pricing signal anywhere** — prospects cannot self-qualify; budget is one optional clarify question.
5. **cosmolabshq@gmail.com as sole channel** visibly conflicts with Fortune-500/federal positioning and the branded domain; no phone/booking/social.
6. **Required phone on wizard step 1** raises the cost of the primary conversion.
7. **No low-commitment path** — full wizard or email only; no capability statement download, no book-a-call, no newsletter.
8. **Funnel silence** across six mid-page sections.
9. **Unsubstantiated proof** — wordmark marquee, anonymous testimonials, hard-coded metrics.
10. **Post-success dead air** — no next action, no draft persistence, no status lookup; skip/build-failed prospects are never told anything.
11. **legal.html English-only** while the funnel is trilingual.
12. Page weight: 236 KB HTML (all CSS/JS/3 dictionaries inline) + ~300 KB images + fonts from Google CDN (render-blocking, third-party).
